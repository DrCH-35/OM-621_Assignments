## üìò Assignment 1 ‚Äî 3-Minute Story & Storyboard  
Assignment 1 required transforming a technical analytics problem into a **clear, compelling narrative** targeted at a professional audience.

### üé§ **Three-Minute Story ‚Äî Summary**
The story focused on a real-world analytics challenge involving transportation delays and forecasting transportation cost behavior across different shipping modes.  

The narrative answered three central questions:

1. **What happened?**  
   Delays and cost fluctuations were identified across shipping modes, impacting operational planning and financial predictability.

2. **Why did it happen?**  
   Insights from exploratory analysis revealed key drivers such as travel distance, mode type, variability in lead times, and external performance inconsistencies.

3. **Why does it matter?**  
   Variability in transportation performance directly affects inventory levels, service reliability, and total landed cost.  
   Improving forecasting accuracy helps organizations plan better, reduce waste, and optimize resources.

### üóÇ Storyboard Elements  
The storyboard provided a visual communication plan for guiding executives through the analysis:

- **Scene 1:** Problem introduction ‚Äî transportation variability and cost uncertainty  
- **Scene 2:** Data landscape ‚Äî where data originated and what variables were relevant  
- **Scene 3:** Key insights ‚Äî distribution of delays, comparison of modes, and variability  
- **Scene 4:** Recommendation ‚Äî strategic actions using analytics to improve forecasting  
- **Scene 5:** Path forward ‚Äî tools and processes for continued optimization  

This storyboard guided the structure of the Python analysis and Power BI visualizations included in Assignments 2 & 3.
